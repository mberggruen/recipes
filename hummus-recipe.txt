Ingredients
  -2c. drained chickpeas, liquid reserved
  -1/2c. tahini
  -1/4c. extra-virgin olive oil
  -2 cloves garlic
  -salt and pepper to taste
  -1T paprika
  -juice of 1 lemon
 
 Preparation
   -Process all ingredients in food processor until smooth, adjust consistency to suit with reserved chickpea liquid.
